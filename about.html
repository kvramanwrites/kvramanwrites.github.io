ABOUT K V RAMAN

K V Raman is a debutante author with a passion for weaving stories that bridge the worlds of science, technology, and human intrigue. With a background in commerce and a lifelong fascination for speculative fiction, Raman’s writing explores the frontiers where scientific discovery meets the mysteries of the human spirit.

His first novel, The Whisper Protocol, is the opening chapter of the Genome Project Trilogy—a genre-bending sci-fi thriller that delves into genetic conspiracies, secret organizations, and the ethical questions of our technological age. Inspired by classic science fiction and the pulse of modern India, Raman crafts narratives that are both thought-provoking and relentlessly suspenseful.

When he isn’t writing, Raman enjoys exploring new advancements in science, reading about ancient civilizations, and pondering the “what ifs” that lie just beyond the edge of possibility. The Whisper Protocol marks the beginning of his journey as an author, and he invites readers to join him as he uncovers the secrets of the Genome Project.
