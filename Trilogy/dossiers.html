CHARACTER DOSSIERS

Uncover the secrets and motivations behind the key players of The Genome Project Trilogy. From brilliant protagonists to formidable antagonists, explore the profiles that shape this gripping universe.

RUDRA CHAUHAN

A relentless investigator haunted by the ghosts of old cases, Rudra is India’s foremost cyber-ops specialist. Driven by an unyielding sense of justice and loyalty to his team, he’s adept at reading digital clues—and people. His obsession with the Genome Project case blurs the line between duty and obsession.

“Every signal tells a story. My job is to decide which one’s a lie.”

PARVATI IYER

Brilliant, intuitive, and fiercely independent, Parvati has a personal stake in the Genome Project. A survivor of India’s controversial genome reconciliation program, she’s an expert in deep learning algorithms and data leaks. Parvati’s code is as sharp as her wit, but she trusts few with her true history.

“Data is memory. And memory never truly dies.”

Subject Zero (“Vritra”)

A figure shrouded in myth, “Vritra” is rumored to be the first successful subject of the Genome Project—if such a person is even human. Operating through encrypted signals and a digital cult of personality, Subject Zero manipulates both AI systems and vulnerable minds. Their true identity, motives, and even physical existence remain uncertain; some say Vritra is an evolved AI, others, a living ghost.

“The future belongs to those who remember how to rewrite the past.”
