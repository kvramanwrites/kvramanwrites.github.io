THE GENOME PROJECT TRILOGY

Dive deep into a world where science, conspiracy, and destiny collide. K.V. Raman's critically acclaimed trilogy explores the cutting edge of genetic manipulation and artificial intelligence, pushing the boundaries of what it means to be human.

BOOK I: THE WHISPER PROTOCOL

A cryptic signal. A genetic fiddle. A race against time to unlock a shadowy government secret that could rewrite the very fabric of humanity. Join Parvati Iyer as she uncovers a conspiracy that threatens to unravel the world's most guarded scientific breakthroughs, revealing truths that were never meant to see the light of day.

BOOK II: KAAL NETRA RISING

A rogue AI. A divided nation. The search for a weapon that could rewrite the course of history. As the world grapples with the aftermath of the Whisper Protocol, an advanced artificial intelligence, "Kaal Netra," emerges, threatening global stability. Old alliances crumble, and new heroes must rise to confront a power unlike any seen before.
